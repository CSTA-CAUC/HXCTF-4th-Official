Internetwache CTF 2016 writeup Crypto 50

This time the games are easier and more interesting than the games I had played in 2016.I achieve the crypto 50 and web 90,I’d like to share my write ups with all of you.

Crypto 50

Description: Did the East German Secret Police see a Pirat on the sky? Help me find out! Hint: We had 9 planets from 1930–2006… Hint2: Each planet has a number. (There’s a table on a well-known website). After that you might be interested in ciphers used by the secret police.

At first, one of my teammates finded the ‘well-known’ website,ok,it’s wikipedia,he finded that the symbols provided represent the nine planets of solar system,and he transformed the symbols to a series of number.

And then,I googled ‘East German Secret Police Cipher’ and  finded out a cipher method which is similar to the form of numbers above,it was ‘TAPIR’,I quickly finded a TAPIR to decode these numbers.

I got this:

-.- –.. ..-. …. .– – – ..-. — …- … … -.-. -..- ..— ..- –. .- -.– …. -.-. -..- ..— -.. — –.. … .– ….- –.. …– … .-.. ….. .– –. . ..— -.-. –… -. –.. … -..- . — .-. .— .–. ..- -…- -…- -…- -…- -…- -…-

Yes,it is MORSE CODE,I decoded it and got this:

KZFHWTTFMVSSCX2UGAYHCX2DOZSW4Z3SL5WGE2C7NZSXEORJPU

This series is formed by Upper alphas and numbers,it is typical BASE32.I just decode this,and get:

VJ{Neee!_T00q_Cvengr_lbh_ner:)}

God!I spotted the difference of VJ and IW,I used rot-13 to decode this,get the flag:

IW{Arrr!_G00d_Pirate_you_are:)}
